Blue Noodle - Molecule Madness

Learn about the Green House Gases!

Water Vapor

Occurs in our atmosphere.

Carbon Dioxide

Occurs when people & animals breathe.
Plants & trees absorb it to live.

Methane

Most plentiful greenhouse gas.
It can come from wetlands and livestock.

Nitrous Oxide

Can come from dead plants.

Ozone

Found naturally in the atmosphere.
